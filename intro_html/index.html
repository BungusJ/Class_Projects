Fun Facts about the Lord of the Rings Movies

Christopher Lee, who played Saruman, was a huge fan of the Lord of the Rings books. So much so that he often corrected Peter Jackson (the director of the movies) on the lore. He was such a devoted fan that even read the books annually. Lee was also the only cast member to have actually meet J.R.R. Tolkien himself. And if that wasn't enough, he even corrected Peter Jackson on what a realistic stabbing to the back looks like based off his real life experience and intelligence of WWII.

In the Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers, actor Viggo Mortensen, who plays Aragorn, kicks a helment and screams out of frustration and greif. This scene was incredibly emotional and was huge staple in this movies recognition. What most people don't know is that Mortensen's scream was completely real. Whenever he kicked the heavy metal helmet, the actor actually broke two of his toes. But instead of immediately calling attention to his injury, Mortensen stayed in character while letting out a genuine scream of agaony. Peter Jackson thought the scene was so awe-inspiring that he left in it the movie. And it is still to this day one of the most iconic scenes in the trilogy.
