About Elden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree

Shadow of the Erdtree is the major expansion for Elden Ring, released in 2024, that deepens the game’s story and challenges. Set in a new region called the Land of Shadow, the DLC takes players beyond the familiar Lands Between into a darker, more mysterious realm tied to the origins of the Erdtree itself. The expansion introduces new enemies, weapons, and bosses, each designed to test even veteran players’ skills. Its atmosphere is haunting and somber, filled with burned fields, shadowed ruins, and eerie golden light that hints at lost divinity. Through exploration, players uncover the secrets of Miquella, one of the most enigmatic demigods in Elden Ring’s lore.

The gameplay in Shadow of the Erdtree pushes the limits of what made Elden Ring great. It builds upon the open-world freedom of the base game but adds a more focused narrative that reveals untold truths about the gods and their followers. New combat mechanics and weapon types, such as martial arts-style attacks and sacred relics, offer fresh ways to play. The expansion also features some of the toughest and most cinematic boss battles FromSoftware has ever created. Ultimately, Shadow of the Erdtree captures the essence of discovery and despair that defines the series—rewarding players who dare to step into the unknown and uncover the shadow behind the light.
